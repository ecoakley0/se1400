Ghost of Tsushima - Audience Analysis

Target Audience

The target audience for Ghost of Tsushima is primarily action-adventure game enthusiasts, particularly those who enjoy open-world exploration and immersive storytelling. The game is rated M for Mature, indicating that it is intended for players aged 17 and older due to its violence and mature themes. The game may also appeal to fans of samurai culture and Japanese history, as it is set in feudal Japan during the Mongol invasion.

Audience Demographics

  • Age: 17-35 years old
