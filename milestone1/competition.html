Ghost of Tsushima - Competition Analysis

What Competing Games do well

  • The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt has a very deep and engaging story that keeps players invested in the game.
  • Red Dead Redemption 2 has a vast open world that allows players to explore and interact with the environment in a variety of ways.
  • Assassin's Creed Valhalla has a strong focus on historical accuracy and immersion, which appeals to players interested in history.

What Competing Games do poorly

  • The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt has a steep learning curve that can be intimidating for new players.
  • Red Dead Redemption 2 has a slow-paced gameplay that may not appeal to players looking for more action.
  • Assassin's Creed Valhalla has been criticized for its repetitive missions and lack of innovation compared to previous entries in the series.

How Ghost of Tsushima can compete

  • Ghost of Tsushima can focus on its unique setting and cultural elements to differentiate itself from other games in the genre.
  • The game can emphasize its combat system, which combines traditional samurai swordplay with stealth mechanics, to provide a fresh gameplay experience.
  • Ghost of Tsushima can also invest in creating a compelling story that resonates with players and keeps them engaged throughout the game.
